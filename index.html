About The Event

About The Event

The expanse of life is so great that it can only be nurtured,maintained and developed through creativity, sensitivity and culture or in a nutshell expression of one's thoughts through art and language . Believing in this principle of life, B.P. Poddar Institute of Management and Technology,Kolkata brings to you its fifth edition of art and language Fest- Panache 2020 wherein the motto will be to compete, learn, experience and enjoy through the precincts of English communication- all that which will turn every individual and a student into a person of great potential and eminence. Our theme for this year "Passion for Expression" encourages one's thoughts, ambitions and actions to be aligned with his or her goal. BPPIMTians look forward to host this grand event on 7th & 8th of April 2020 with flamboyant confidence, style and manner which are the marks of the modern times.The BPPIMT family thus invites all to be a part of their endeavour to make this event a grand success. So come and join us on this quest of passion and adventure with The Panache 2020.
